# Retail Customer Churn Dashboard

## Overview
This project analyzes customer churn, engagement, loyalty, and revenue trends using an interactive Tableau dashboard. The dashboard was designed to identify behavioral patterns associated with customer retention and churn risk while providing clear business insights through KPI-driven visualizations.

The analysis focuses on customer segmentation, regional revenue performance, engagement metrics, and retention behavior to support data-driven decision-making.

---

## Tools Used
- Tableau

---

## Dashboard Features
- Interactive KPI cards displaying:
  - Total Customers
  - Churn Rate
  - Total Revenue
  - Average Order Total
  - Average Loyalty Score
  - Average Engagement Score

- Dynamic visualizations including:
  - Churn by Customer Segment
  - Revenue by Region
  - Loyalty vs. Engagement Analysis
  - Retention by Channel

- Interactive filters for:
  - Region
  - Customer Segment
  - Age Group
  - Churn Risk

---

## Key Insights
- Customers with lower engagement and loyalty scores demonstrated significantly higher churn rates.
- Revenue performance varied significantly across regions, with a difference of over $3 million between the highest and lowest performing regions.
- Churn rates remained relatively consistent across customer segments, suggesting that churn behavior may be influenced more by individual customer behavior than by segment classification alone.
- Customer interaction channels showed minimal variation in churn rates, indicating similar retention performance across channels.

---

## Calculated Fields
Several Tableau calculated fields were created to support the dashboard analysis, including:
- Churn Rate
- Engagement Category
- Loyalty Tier
- Estimated Customer Lifetime Value (CLV)
